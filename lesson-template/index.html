About me



My name is Ian Spangler and I'm a digital cultural geographer who's interested in the relationships between platform capitalism, social difference, and the city. I'm a second-year PhD student at the University of Kentucky. I mainly write about gentrification, tourism, and short-term rental platforms like Airbnb. I've also written about distortion, both in music and in maps.

Thanks for vibing it and keeping it tight. If you need me, I'm on my mobile.
